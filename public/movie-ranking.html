The Best Movies According to Aarya

My top 3 movies of all time.

Wake Up Sid

A coming-of-age story about a careless, privileged college boy named Sid who learns responsibility and discovers his passion for photography after moving in with an independent writer, Aisha. It’s warm, relatable, and all about growing up.

Frankenstein

Based on Mary Shelley’s iconic story, it follows a scientist, Victor Frankenstein, who creates a living creature from dead body parts. The creature, misunderstood and rejected by society, becomes both tragic and terrifying. A classic tale about ambition, loneliness, and humanity.

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

A fun rom-com where Andie writes an article about how to make a guy leave her in 10 days, while Ben tries to make her fall in love with him for a bet. Their plans clash in hilarious ways, leading to unexpected romance.
